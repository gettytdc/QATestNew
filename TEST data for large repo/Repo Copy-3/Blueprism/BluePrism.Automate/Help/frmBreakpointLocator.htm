Locating and Managing Breakpoints

The breakpoint locator form allows you to find and manage your breakpoints. Breakpoints are listed in the treeview on the left sorted by name or by page, according to your preference set in the checkbox.

Any condition set on an existing breakpoint will be displayed in the box on the right; it will not be editable.

Individual breakpoints may be deleted by selecting the item in the treeview and clicking the "Clear" button. All breakpoints can be cleared at once by clicking the "Clear All" button at any time These changes are not reversible.

If you wish to see the stage at which a breakpoint was created, you may simply double click the item in the treeview, or you may select it and click the "Go to Breakpoint" button.
