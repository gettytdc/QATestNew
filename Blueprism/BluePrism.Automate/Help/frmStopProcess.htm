Stop a Process

To stop a single process on a single resource quickly and easily, use the Stop a Process wizard.

Two steps are required to complete the task:

1. Locate a Process

The Process list shows all currently running processes. Click on one of the processes to highlight it - this will be the process to be stopped..

2. Stop Process

Following the selection of a process, selecting the Stop Process link will stop the process at the next available step on the selected resource.
